# HR Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing employee attrition and workforce trends using Power BI. The dashboard provides insights into employee demographics, job roles, salaries, work experience, and factors contributing to attrition. The goal is to help HR teams make data-driven decisions to improve employee retention and workforce management.

---

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- CSV Dataset

---

## Dataset Information
The dataset contains employee-related information including:

- Employee Demographics
- Department Details
- Job Roles
- Monthly Income
- Attrition Status
- Work Experience
- Job Satisfaction
- Work-Life Balance
- Overtime Information
- Promotion History

**Dataset Size:** 1,480 Records × 38 Columns

---

## Key Business Questions Answered

- What is the overall employee attrition rate?
- Which departments have the highest attrition?
- Which job roles experience the most employee turnover?
- How does salary impact attrition?
- What age groups are most likely to leave?
- Does overtime contribute to employee attrition?
- What is the relationship between experience and attrition?

## Key Insights

- Employees working overtime show higher attrition rates.
- Lower salary slabs have higher employee turnover.
- Certain job roles contribute significantly to overall attrition.
- Younger employees are more likely to leave the organization.
- Promotion delays can impact employee retention.
